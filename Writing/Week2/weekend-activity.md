# Week 2 - Weekend Activity: Story Retelling

## Goal
Help Harry practice retelling stories in his own words and writing them down together.

## Activity (20 minutes)

### Step 1: Choose Content (5 mins)
Pick one of these options:
- Watch a short cartoon (5-10 minutes)
- Read a picture book together
- Talk about something that happened this week

### Step 2: Story Retelling (10 mins)
Ask Harry to tell you what happened in 5 sentences:
1. "What happened first?"
2. "Then what happened?"
3. "What was the most important part?"
4. "How did it end?"
5. "What was your favorite part?"

### Step 3: Write Together (5 mins)
- Write down his 5 sentences exactly as he says them
- Don't worry about perfect grammar
- Let him help with spelling if he wants to
- Read it back to him when finished

## Example
**If watching "Peppa Pig":**
1. "Peppa and George went to the playground."
2. "Peppa wanted to go on the big slide but she was scared."
3. "Daddy Pig helped her be brave."
4. "She went down the slide and had fun."
5. "My favorite part was when she laughed at the bottom."

## Success Markers
- Harry can remember the main events in order
- He uses connecting words like "then," "next," "after that"
- He enjoys sharing the story
- He's proud when you write down his words

## Tips
- If he forgets parts, ask: "What do you remember about...?"
- Celebrate his memory: "You remembered so many details!"
- Keep the written story - he can read it again later
