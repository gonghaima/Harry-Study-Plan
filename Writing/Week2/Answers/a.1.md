# Week 2 - Topic 1: Adding Details & "Show Don't Tell"

## Boring vs. Exciting Sentences (10 mins)

### Day 1 - Monday

**Starting Sentence:** "The dog was happy"
**Upgrade to:** 
- His answer: "The dog was excited because he got a bone."

**Practice Words:** "excited", "tired", "hungry"

- Ask him to upgrade: "The boy was excited"
- His answer: "The boy was peppy because it was his birthday"

Enhanced version: "The boy jumped out of bed and grinned—it was finally his birthday!"

## Five Sensese Writing (10 mins)

### Day 1 - Monday

**Topic:** Apple

- **See:** red, shiny, round
- **Hear:** crunch when you bite it
- **Smell:** sweet, fresh
- **Feel:** smooth, cool, hard
- **Taste:** sweet, juicy

Ask him to pick 3 senses and write: ""See / Feel / Smell

- His answer: The red, shiny and round apple smelled fresh plus clean, and also smooth and some parts rough.

Enhanced version: "The red, shiny apple smelled fresh and sweet. Its skin felt smooth and cool, except for a few rough spots near the stem."
